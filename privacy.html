Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Financial Autopsy application ("the App," "we," "us") handles information in connection with its use of the TikTok Content Posting API.

1. Who We Are

Financial Autopsy is an independent educational content channel that produces short-form videos on financial literacy and economic systems. The App is a private tool used solely to publish our own original content to our own TikTok account.

2. Information We Access

The App accesses the minimum information required to publish content to our own TikTok account, including:

  • Basic account information (account name and open ID) to authenticate and confirm the destination account.
  • Authorization tokens provided by TikTok to allow the App to upload and publish video content on our behalf.

The App does not collect, access, or process personal data from any other TikTok users, viewers, or third parties.

3. How We Use Information

Information is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate the App with TikTok.
  • Upload and publish our own video content to our own TikTok account.

We do not use this information for advertising, profiling, resale, or any purpose unrelated to publishing our own content.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any accessed information with third parties. Authorization tokens are stored securely and used only to communicate with TikTok's API.

5. Data Retention

Authorization tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate the App and are deleted upon revocation of access or discontinuation of the App.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored credentials and tokens against unauthorized access. Access is limited to the App operator.

7. Your Rights

Because the App only acts on our own account, no third-party user data is involved. The account owner may revoke the App's access at any time through TikTok account settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact
